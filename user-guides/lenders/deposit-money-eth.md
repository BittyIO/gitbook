# Deposit money (ETH)

1\.    Visit the [Bitty.io](https://bitty.io/lending/ethereum/nft) website, connect your wallet in the top right corner, click "Crypto”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/1 (3).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2\.    Click “Supply”.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/3 (5).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3\.    The “Supply” page will pop up; You can input the “Amount” as you like.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/4 (5).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4\.    Input the “Amount”, click “ok”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/5 (4).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5\.    Your wallet will pop up, confirm your account, click "Crypto", check the “Supplied Amount", check the “Wallet Balance”.&#x20;

If you want to supply all your balance, click “supply more”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/8 (2).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6\.    The “Supply” page will pop up; Click “MAX”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/9 (5).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

7\.    Check “Amount”, click “OK”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/10 (4).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

8\.    Your wallet will pop up, confirm your account, click “Crypto”, check “Supplied Amount”, check “Wallet Balance”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/12 (1).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
