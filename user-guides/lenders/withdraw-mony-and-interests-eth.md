# Withdraw mony & interests (ETH)

1\.    Visit the [Bitty.io](https://bitty.io/lending/ethereum/nft) website, connect your wallet in the top right corner, click "Crypto”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/1 (6).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2\.    Click the three dots next to “Supply more”, click “Withdraw”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/3 (7).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

&#x20;

3\.    The “Withdraw” page will pop up; you can “Enter an amount” as you like.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/4 (6).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4\.    Input the “Amount”, click “OK”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/5 (7).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5\.    Your wallet will pop up, confirm your account, click "Crypto", check the “Supplied Amount”, check the “Wallet Balance”. If you want to withdraw all your balance, click the three dots next to “Supply more”, click “Withdraw”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/8 (6).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6\.    The “Withdraw” page will pop up; click “MAX”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/9 (8).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

7\.    Check “Amount”, click “OK”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/10 (6).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

8\.    Your wallet will pop up, confirm your account, click “Crypto”, check “Supplied Amount”, check “Wallet Balance”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/12 (2).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
