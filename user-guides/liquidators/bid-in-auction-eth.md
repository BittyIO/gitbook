# Bid in auction (ETH)

1\.    Visit the [Bitty.io](https://bitty.io/lending/ethereum/nft) website, connect your wallet in the top right corner, click "Auctions”. &#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/1 (5).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2\.    Click “In Auction” ;There is a NFT in Auction; Click “Bid”.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/2 (6).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3\.    Click “Bid”; Your wallet will pop up, confirm your account.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/3 (6).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4\.    Click “Participated”, check you have participated in auction. If there are no higher bids, you will win this auction.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/5 (6).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5\.    “Congratulations! You have won the auction” means there are no higher bids after you bid. Click “Liquidate”.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/8 (4).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6\.    Click “NFT”, check your wallet balance, increased by 1. The NFT from the auction comes to your wallet.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/9 (7).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
