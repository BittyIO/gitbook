# Tigger an auction as the first bidder (ETH)

1\.    Visit the [Bitty.io](https://bitty.io/lending/ethereum/nft) website, connect your wallet in the top right corner, click "Auctions”. &#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/0 (1).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2\.    There is a NFT which HF below 1; Click “First Bid” to trigger the auction.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/2 (4).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3\.    Your wallet will pop up, confirm your account; "You are currently the highest bidder", click "Auctions".&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/4 (4).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4\.    Click “Participated”, check “Last Bid Time”. If there are no higher bids in the next 12 hours, you will win this auction as the first bidder.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/5 (3).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5\.    “Congratulations! You have won the auction” means there are no higher bids 12 hours after you trigger the bid as the first bidder.

Click “Liquidate”.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/6 (2).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6\.    Click “NFT”, check your wallet balance, increased by 1. The NFT from the auction comes to your wallet.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/7 (2) (1).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
